The Bachelorettes Uncover Marco’s Secret

Posted on July 20, 2016August 15, 2017


After a fun-filled shoot with the Bachelorettes of Zee Lifestyle for this month’s issue, we all gathered again to uncover one of Marco Polo’s well-kept specialty. Can you guess what it is?  Like any girls’ night, everything started with a little “chika” (catching-up).  Contrary to our last meeting wherein we were all dolled-up, we all looked more casual and relaxed until our friends in Game of Thrones costume came along. Dolled up in a different kind of way! See the photo below to be the judge!  If good girls are together then there should be good food ( a lot of it!).
