3 Reasons Why You Should Shop at Beauty MNL

Posted on December 28, 2016April 18, 2017

As women, we are continually on the hunt for the best beauty products. We don’t always get our skin care products right nor pick the lipstick brand that suits us. As a modern career woman, my schedule can get super hectic and this prevents me from checking out the latest products out in the mall and seeing other options. The heavy traffic anywhere we go doesn’t also help!I’m  certain many women can relate to this. One of my latest personal discovery  that I’m truly grateful for is Beauty MNL.

Let me share to you my top reasons as to why I easily got hooked with shopping at this beauty shop site!

1. Shop site interface is very friendly (even to the new users)

Let’s admit it – some shop sites are not that easy to use. I think one of the main things to consider in choosing a shop site regardless of what you are shopping for is its interface. It has to make your shopping experience easier and not more complicated. Although you are a new guest, shopping at Beauty MNL is a piece of cake!

2. Wide variety of products (some you might have not even heard of!)

Aside from the usual beauty products and brands that we already know of, you will find many interesting brands which you have probably not heard of. The best thing about Beauty MNL is that it asks you for your skin type and age – not many beauty sites do this. It’s amusing that many beauty shop sites overlook this when we all know that determining skin types and age greatly affect our choices for the beauty products we use. Beauty MNL also provides the look on different swatches as well as feedback of the previous shoppers of every product and I find this very useful. When I’m feeling adventurous, I try new products and go through the reviews and do extra research outside of the shop site. So far, it has been a great help and I have been happy with my new discoveries!

3. Overall transaction is smooth (fast delivery too!)

The overall transaction is smooth. Although I am residing in Cebu, my package arrived earlier than expected. This is such a relief since I have had my fair share of orders arriving super late.  I also like the fact that the packaging was done so well. Every item was wrapped with a bubble wrap to prevent breakage or damage.  It sounds like something online sellers should strictly follow but not everyone adhere to this. I experienced receiving a damaged cosmetic item. Nobody wants to be disappointed because of a damaged item.

If you are curious on what are the last items I got, here’s the list. I highly recommend all of these items (without bias!) These are mostly affordable items but these are my top 3:

  • Lip and Cheek Tint by Skin Genie does wonders to your cheeks! It smells so good as well.
  • The Skin Genie Epsom salt is a must! So refreshing!
  • The Facial Pearl Cream with Sunscreen which costs less than Php 50 is unbelievably good. Just choose the shade that fits your skin type.

Other items: ( All still highly recommended – please note that this is based on my personal preference and experience)

  • Skin Beautifying Day Tonic 
  • LA Colors 3D Blush
  • Voluminous Million Lashes Mascara by L’oreal Paris 
  • Suesh Concealer 
  • Golden Cup Oil 

Now that the holiday rush is almost over – you can start thinking about your date looks for Valentines! I’m quite certain you will find your new favorite skin care or make-up item at Beauty MNL.
