Where is Ilaputi Moving?

Posted on July 24, 2017August 15, 2017


One of our favorite dining places in I.T. Park is moving. Wait, what?

I have so many precious memories at Ilaputi. I bet the same goes for you!

In 2010, I remember my classmates and I would treat ourselves with The Stroganov after long hours of review for board exams. It then became a go to place for dinner with work mates. I’ve also had some memorable romantic dates here. Oops!

For 10 solid years, Ilaputi has served us with our favorites The Stroganov, Dragon, and Angus Goes Dimsum ,etc. on the ground floor of the i1 Building.

This September, Ilaputi will be making a big move! Your favorites will still be served but this time, you have to head over to The Greenery, Pope John Paul II Road, in Barangay Kasambagan, Cebu City.

To mark this momentous occasion, Ilaputi’s I.T. Park branch will hold a Grand Moving Party on Saturday, July 29.

Starting at 8:00 P.M., a program will kick off Ilaputi’s Grand Moving Party with special activities to celebrate the I.T. Park branch’s decade-long service, alongside a live band.

Diners who drop their business cards or leave their contact details at the registration table are automatically entered into the grand raffle for a chance to win Ilaputi gift certificates — Php 1,000 for the third prize, Php 2,000 for the second prize, and Php 3,000 for the top prize.


Throughout the night, there will be more opportunities to win gift certificates. Trivia questions will be asked about Ilaputi in general, and its I.T. Park branch in particular, and simple parlor games will also be conducted. Each diner to correctly answer a trivia question or successfully complete a task will receive a gift certificate worth P500, which may be redeemed at Ilaputi at The Greenery when it opens.

From now until July 28, contests will also be held through Ilaputi’s Facebook and Instagram social media accounts. To participate, be sure to follow Ilaputi on Facebook and on Instagram.

Ilaputi in Cebu I.T. Park will continue to open at its regular hours of 11 a.m. to 2 a.m. until July 30.

All regular menu items will also continue to be served until moving day.
