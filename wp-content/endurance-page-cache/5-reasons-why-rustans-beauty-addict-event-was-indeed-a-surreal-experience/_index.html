5 Reasons Why Rustan’s Beauty Addict Event Was Indeed a Surreal Experience

Posted on November 20, 2016August 15, 2017


Women’s addiction to beauty products has been inscribed in the history of mankind. Make-up has become a weapon for women – a weapon to highlight one’s beauty, become more confident and ready to conquer the world. Cleopatra who ruled a kingdom pretty much invented eyeliner and had the most iconic cat’s eye up to now! For a modern day woman, she also rules her own kingdom – whether it be managing her household or nailing her corporate tasks. Every woman needs to know what make-up brands suit her skin and what look fits her perfectly.

When we talk about beauty and make-up brands, Rustan’s is probably the first place in your mind that has everything you need and want!  Last November 16, 2016, Rustan’s Cebu hosted an event for beauty addicts.

Here are 5 reasons why it was indeed a surreal event!

1. So many luxurious brands! 

The long line-up of luxurious brands is impeccable. From Bobbi Brown to Yves Saint Laurent to Chanel and all other brands you can think of are all there. Each of the brands had a specific activity or game that we participated on to win a prize. Some required you to take selfies while some had origami making, beer pong,etc. It was super fun!

2. Lots of exciting prizes (and freebies too!) 

As mentioned, each of the brands had a different activity or game. It was amusing and cool to see women exert all their efforts to get their hands on their prizes.  Who wouldn’t be motivated with NARS orgasm blush or a facial from LA PRAIRIE and other exciting products?

For LAURA MERCIER, the ladies were asked to pose for a photo at the ring light and upload it to their respective social media accounts. This ring light is everything!

3. Good company and party!  

Events such as this celebrate women! It brings women together from different walks of life who share the same passion, love and addiction with make-up and beauty products.  (Oh and free flowing Belvedere and Moët too!)


4. It’s a gift that keeps giving! 

It was a beauty event with a cause. A charity photo booth was set-up wherein for every photo taken there, Rustan’s gives back to its chosen charity, the iCanServe Foundation. They help women with Breast cancer.

5. JO MALONE is finally here!

Jo Malone London made its way to Cebu – finally! For those who are perfume lovers, this brand is very popular but did you know that this UK based fragrance designer started out as a florist? She loves fragrant flowers and this is how it all began. It was in 1994 when she finally opened a flagship boutique in London. Its simple but elegant packaging already tells a lot about its scent. What’s your favorite Jo Malone scent? ( Nectarine Blossom & Honey for me!)
